How to Use

  1. Add a book or show

    Enter a title, choose Book or Show, and optionally note why you're interested.

  2. Mark it finished

    Tap the status badge on a card to switch between "Want to" and "Finished".

  3. Rate it & reflect

    Once finished, give it a star rating and write down what you learned.

  4. Filter your list

    Use the pill buttons above the list to narrow it down by type or status.
